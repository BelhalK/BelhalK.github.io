    I am currently a Ph.D. student at Ecole Polytechnique (CMAP), under the supervision of Marc Lavielle and Eric Moulines. My research interests lie in the fields of high dimensional statistics and machine learning. My current work focuses on the acceleration and optimization of training algorithms for large datasets. I am, in particular, working on Maximum Likelihood estimation algorithms for mixed effects models. Prior that, I've visited the Massachusetts Institute of Technology where I spend most of my time at the Probabilistic Computing Project working on MCMC methods.
    I've been using data science and machine learning techniques in various domains such as :

    Social Sciences: Telecom ParisTech - Behaviors toward private data disclosure
    Economics: Agoranov - Job creation impact of startups
    Education: MIT - US College Scorecard
    Growth & Development: Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation - HBGDki

    At the moment, I am mainly interested in Machine Learning and Computational Social Science.
    If you find something interesting to you here, feel free to reach out !
